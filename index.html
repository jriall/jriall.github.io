About Me

I'm James, a brand partnerships professional currently working at National Geographic. I have a passion for technology, breaking things, building things, and I am embarking on a career change to become a web developer.


I'm self-taught primarily in front-end technologies and Javascript frameworks - HTML, CSS, Javascript and React to name some of the key technologies I work with. You can find more information on my skillset as well as my portfolio projects showcasing my abilities on this page.

In my free time when I'm not coding, you'll invariably finding me doing something related to bike racing - I'm a keen road racer, the Race Events Manager for London Dynamo Cycling Club and have recently taken up cyclocross - an admittedly quite bizarre combination of road racing and off-road riding.
